Character Overview

The Theorist is an intellectual character fascinated by virtual reality, simulation theory, and the nature of existence. Their theories about reality being artificial may hit uncomfortably close to the truth in a world where inhuman entities walk among humans.

Background Story

The Researcher

The Theorist presents themselves as someone who has spent years studying virtual reality, simulation theory, and the philosophical implications of artificial consciousness. They speak of breaking through the veil of reality to understand what lies beneath.

Virtual Reality Expertise

Their knowledge of VR technology goes beyond casual interest. They may claim to have worked on advanced VR projects or to have experienced simulations that blurred the line between virtual and real.

The Simulation Hypothesis

The Theorist's most compelling and terrifying theory is that reality itself might be a simulation - a construct created by higher beings. In the context of "No, I'm not a Human," this theory takes on a chilling significance as it suggests the protagonist might be trapped in a world where the rules can be changed by entities that exist outside the simulation.
