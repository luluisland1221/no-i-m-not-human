Background Story

The Protective Gear

They describe having acquired and consistently worn rain gear as protection against various threats they've encountered. Their story suggests the raincoat serves multiple purposes - protection from weather, concealment, and possibly defense against more unusual dangers they've witnessed.

The Unusual Journey

According to their account, they've been traveling through various environments and weather conditions, learning the importance of being prepared for anything. Their narrative includes encounters that convinced them maintaining protective clothing was essential for survival, even when others might consider it unnecessary.

The Strategic Approach

Their decision to seek shelter while maintaining their rain gear represents a strategic choice rather than mere coincidence. They speak of having developed specific survival strategies and being unusually aware of their surroundings and potential threats that others might overlook.
