Background Story

Weather-Protected Child

The Raincoat Child appears to be a young person prepared for inclement weather. His raincoat suggests he may have been traveling in poor conditions or is expecting bad weather, indicating he might be lost, running away, or seeking shelter.

Purpose for Visiting

His reasons for seeking entry may relate to needing shelter from storms, escaping dangerous weather conditions, or being separated from guardians. His childlike vulnerability makes his need for protection seem more urgent.

Behavioral Traits

He maintains a youthful demeanor that encourages adult protection and care, but this apparent innocence might be masking supernatural abilities or hiding his true nature behind a facade of childhood vulnerability.
