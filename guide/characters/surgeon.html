Character Overview

The Surgeon appears as a medical professional offering help and medical assistance. However, in the world of "No, I'm not a Human," even those who seem to offer healing may hide terrifying secrets beneath their professional exterior.

Professional Appearance

Appears as a trained medical expert with surgical knowledge

Healing Intentions

Claims to want to help with medical needs and injuries

Suspicious Nature

True motivations and identity remain questionable

Background Story

Medical Background

The Surgeon presents themselves as a trained medical professional with extensive surgical experience. They may claim to have worked in hospitals, emergency rooms, or specialized medical facilities.

The Horror Element

In a world where anyone could be an inhuman entity, the Surgeon's medical knowledge becomes particularly chilling. Their surgical skills could be used for healing... or for something far more sinister.

Hidden Motives

Like all characters in the game, the Surgeon may not be what they appear. Their offer of medical help could be a ruse to gain trust, access, or something more nefarious.
