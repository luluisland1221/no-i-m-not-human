Character Overview

The Intruder is the main antagonist and mascot of the game, appearing as a tall figure with pale skin, broad shoulders, and freakishly long, skinny arms. He wears dark pants and has short black hair with an eerie grin. He is consistently the most dangerous and powerful visitor, serving as a major threat to the player.

First Impression

Pale skin, unsettling grin, and strangely folded skin that appears "too big for him"

Key Behavior

Violent, takes joy in murder, but selectively targets homeowners who are alone

Unique Nature

The only visitor that the player cannot kill, appears on specific nights

Background Story

Forced Entry

The Intruder approaches with hostile intentions, attempting to gain entry through force rather than persuasion. This could include breaking windows, picking locks, or attempting to physically overpower the protagonist.

Unknown Motives

The intruder's motives may vary - from simple burglary to more sinister intentions related to the game's central mystery. Their true nature as human or visitor adds another layer of danger to the encounter.
