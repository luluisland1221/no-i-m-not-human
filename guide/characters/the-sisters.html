Character Overview

The Sisters are a mysterious pair of visitors who appear together, seemingly sharing a deep, possibly psychic connection. They move and speak in unnerving synchronicity, creating an atmosphere of supernatural unease.

Dual Presence

Always appears as a pair, never alone

Synchronized Behavior

Exhibit identical movements and speech patterns

Mysterious Connection

Share an unexplained bond beyond normal sibling relationships

Background Story

The Twin Mystery

The Sisters claim to be twins with an extraordinary connection, but their story grows increasingly strange as they interact with the protagonist. They may speak of shared dreams, simultaneous thoughts, or even shared physical sensations.

Supernatural Elements

Some accounts suggest that The Sisters might be manifestations of a single entity or beings that exist across multiple planes of reality. Their synchronized behavior goes beyond normal twin telepathy into something more disturbing.

The Truth Behind the Bond

As the protagonist interacts with them, it becomes clear that their connection may not be natural. They could be entities that have taken human form or sisters who have undergone some kind of transformation that links their very existence.
