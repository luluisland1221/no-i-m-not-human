Character Overview

The Sisters are dual entities who move and think as one unit. They appear as female figures with an uncanny synchronicity in their movements and speech, suggesting a supernatural connection that transcends normal human sibling relationships.

Supernatural Bond

Shares thoughts and movements with an otherworldly connection

Background Story

Mysterious Origin

The Sisters appear to have been born from a supernatural event or phenomenon, their connection forming before birth. They share memories and experiences that neither could have acquired individually, suggesting their bond transcends normal human existence.

Shared Existence

Their purpose appears to be finding others who understand their unique connection or seeking to expand their collective consciousness. They move through the world as one entity, experiencing reality simultaneously through two separate physical forms.

Otherworldly Nature

Their synchronized behavior extends beyond normal coordination, indicating they may not be entirely human. They communicate without speaking and respond to external stimuli as if processing information through a shared mind.
