Character Overview

The Vigilante is a self-proclaimed neighborhood protector who takes justice into their own hands. Known for carrying weapons and trophies from their victims, this character represents one of the most dangerous and morally ambiguous encounters in the game.

First Impression

Intimidating presence with visible weapons and trophies

Key Behavior

Claims to protect the neighborhood through violent means

Moral Complexity

Blurs the line between protector and predator

Background Story

Self-Appointed Protector

The Vigilante presents themselves as a necessary force in the neighborhood, claiming to protect residents from the visitor threat through extrajudicial means. Their methods are violent and their morality questionable.

Notable Encounters

According to the Wounded Man's testimony, the Vigilante is described as "a guy with a shotgun on his back, necklace of teeth and fingers." This suggests they collect trophies from their victims, raising questions about their true motives and methods.
