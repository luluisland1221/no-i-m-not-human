Character Overview

The Child is a young visitor who appears as a child, creating one of the most morally challenging encounters in the game. This character tests players' emotional responses and decision-making abilities.

First Impression

Appears as an innocent child seeking help or shelter

Key Behavior

May use innocence as a tool for manipulation or genuine need

Moral Challenge

Creates difficult ethical decisions about helping children

Background Story

Arrival

The Child appears at the door with various stories - lost, in trouble, or seeking help. The innocence of a child makes these stories particularly compelling and difficult to question.

True Nature

Like all visitors, the Child's true nature is randomly determined. This creates intense moral dilemmas for players who must balance compassion with caution.
