Background Story

Life with Cats

The Cat Lady has devoted her life to caring for felines, often preferring their company to humans. Her home is filled with cats, and she claims they communicate with her in ways others cannot understand.

Social Isolation

Her intense focus on cats has led to increasing social withdrawal, making her interactions with humans awkward and unpredictable. She sees cats as more trustworthy than people.

Unusual Abilities

She insists on having special connections with felines, claiming they tell her secrets and warn her of dangers. These claims make others question her mental state.
