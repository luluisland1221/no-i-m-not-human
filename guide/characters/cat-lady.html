Character Overview

The Cat Lady is an elderly woman visitor known for her love of cats. She represents one of the more seemingly innocent visitors that players may encounter, creating interesting identification challenges.

First Impression

Appears as a harmless elderly woman with cat-related interests

Key Behavior

May talk about her cats and appear as a typical neighborhood resident

Identification Challenge

Innocent appearance makes non-human detection difficult

Background Story

Neighborhood Resident

The Cat Lady presents herself as a local resident who loves cats and may be seeking help for her feline companions or herself. Her elderly demeanor creates an air of authenticity.

Varied Motives

Her reasons for seeking entry can range from genuine need to more suspicious intentions. As with all visitors, her true nature is randomly determined, creating different scenarios for players to navigate.
