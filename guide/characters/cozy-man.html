Character Overview

The Cozy Man is a male visitor who appears comfortable and relaxed in his demeanor. His seemingly harmless and approachable nature makes him an interesting challenge for players trying to identify potential visitors.

First Impression

Appears relaxed, comfortable, and non-threatening

Key Behavior

Exhibits calm, casual behavior that puts people at ease

Identification Challenge

Relaxed demeanor makes detection of non-human traits difficult

Background Story

Casual Approach

The Cozy Man approaches with a relaxed, casual attitude that makes him seem like a normal, friendly neighbor. His comfortable demeanor helps lower suspicions and makes his requests for entry seem more reasonable.

Hidden Nature

Like all visitors, his true nature is randomly determined. His cozy appearance may be genuine or a clever disguise to hide his true identity as a visitor.
