Character Overview

Nikita the Wind is an elemental being composed of air and wind currents, capable of infiltrating spaces that should be impenetrable. This "Based Intruder" represents a different type of Visitor threat - one that doesn't mimic human form but embodies natural forces.

First Impression

Appears as a shimmering figure that seems to flow around objects rather than walk through them

Key Behavior

Moves with unnatural fluidity, often appears suddenly without warning

Elemental Nature

Speaks of winds, breezes, and air currents as if they were extensions of self

Background Story

The Living Wind

Nikita claims to be an ancient consciousness that has always existed within the wind currents of the world. According to them, they are not an intruder in the human sense, but a natural force that has gained self-awareness and curiosity about human structures and lives.

Elemental Awakening

The entity speaks of a great awakening among the natural elements, where wind, water, fire, and earth have begun to develop individual consciousnesses. Nikita represents the wind's manifestation, drawn to human dwellings by the emotional and spiritual energy they contain.

The Intrusion

Nikita doesn't understand human concepts of private property or boundaries. To them, air flows freely through all spaces, and their movement through locked doors and sealed windows is as natural as a breeze through an open window. They genuinely don't comprehend why humans find their presence unsettling.
