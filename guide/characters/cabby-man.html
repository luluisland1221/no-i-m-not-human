Character Overview

The Cabby Man presents himself as a seasoned taxi driver with extensive knowledge of the city and its inhabitants. His profession has supposedly given him insight into the lives of countless passengers, making him a potentially valuable source of information about the strange occurrences in the neighborhood.

Professional Demeanor

Exhibits characteristics of someone who has spent years behind the wheel - observational, street-smart, and seemingly trustworthy

Information Broker

Claims to know secrets about various residents and visitors due to his profession

Street Wisdom

Demonstrates knowledge of city layouts, routes, and local gossip

Background Story

The Professional

Claims to have been driving a taxi for many years, accumulating stories and knowledge about the city's residents, including those involved in the mysterious events plaguing the neighborhood.

The Observer

His position as a taxi driver has supposedly given him unique insights into the lives of others. He may claim to have picked up suspicious characters, overheard concerning conversations, or witnessed strange behavior from various residents.

The Questionable Past

Despite his seemingly legitimate profession, questions remain about his true motives. Is he genuinely seeking refuge, or is his presence part of a larger investigation or scheme?
