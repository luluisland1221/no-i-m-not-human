Background Story

Transportation Background

The Cabby Man has spent years driving through city streets, encountering countless passengers from all walks of life. His profession has given him unique insights into urban life and human behavior.

Information Network

Through his work, he has developed an extensive network of contacts and gathered information about various people and locations. This makes him either a valuable ally or a potential threat.

Survival Skills

His experience navigating dangerous neighborhoods and dealing with difficult situations has honed his street smarts and ability to read people quickly and accurately.
