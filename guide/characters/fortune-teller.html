Character Overview

The Fortune Teller is a mystical visitor who claims to have supernatural abilities to see the future and read fortunes. This character represents the game's exploration of supernatural elements and the blurred line between genuine psychic ability and visitor deception.

First Impression

Mysterious, mystical appearance with claims of supernatural knowledge

Key Behavior

Offers prophecies, readings, and mystical insights

Supernatural Element

Represents the game's supernatural and mysterious aspects

Background Story

Mystical Arrival

The Fortune Teller arrives claiming to have had visions or premonitions about the protagonist or the neighborhood situation. They may offer warnings, predictions, or insights about the visitor threat.

Supernatural Claims

Their claims of supernatural abilities could be genuine psychic powers, elaborate deception, or evidence of their true nature as a non-human entity with otherworldly knowledge.
