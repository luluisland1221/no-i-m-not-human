Character Overview

The Protagonist is the player character in "No, I'm not a Human", working as a doorman/apartment manager during a series of supernatural events. Players must identify various visitors while managing their own survival and uncovering the mysteries surrounding their building.

Player Control

The character directly controlled by the player throughout the game

Key Role

Survival Focus

Must balance visitor management with personal survival needs

Background Story

Mining Background

The protagonist has a background in mining, which may explain their attention to detail and methodical approach to visitor screening. This profession likely provided them with the observational skills needed for their current role.

Doorman Position

Currently working as a doorman or apartment manager, responsible for screening all visitors to the building. This position places them at the center of the supernatural events unfolding in the game.
