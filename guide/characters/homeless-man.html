Character Overview

The Homeless Man is a desperate visitor who appears at the protagonist's door seeking refuge from the harsh conditions of street life. His appearance and demeanor suggest years of hardship and survival on the margins of society.

First Impression

Presents as a victim of circumstance, seeking shelter and basic necessities

Key Behavior

Shares survival stories and street wisdom, may offer valuable information

Survival Instinct

Adapted to life on the streets, resourceful and observant

Background Story

Life on the Streets

The Homeless Man claims to have spent years surviving on the streets, witnessing the gradual deterioration of society and the rise of strange occurrences. His body bears the marks of harsh living, and his eyes reflect countless horrors seen in alleyways and abandoned buildings.

Knowledge and Wisdom

Through his transient existence, he has gathered information about various groups, safe zones, and the true nature of the Visitors that plague the city. He understands that appearances can be deceiving and that trust is a luxury few can afford.

Current Situation

Recent events have made street survival increasingly difficult, forcing him to seek refuge with those who might offer shelter. He claims that other homeless individuals have been disappearing or behaving strangely, suggesting that even the marginalized are not safe from the Visitor threat.
