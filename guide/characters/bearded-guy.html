Background Story

Traumatic Experiences

The Bearded Guy may have experienced traumatic events that led to his paranoid state. His distrustful nature suggests past betrayals, dangerous encounters, or situations that have taught him to be wary of others.

Survival Mentality

His behavior indicates a strong survival instinct, where trust is seen as a liability rather than an asset. This mindset may have developed from living in dangerous or uncertain conditions.

He may be seeking safety or refuge while maintaining his defensive posture. His request for entry might be driven by desperation, but his suspicious nature makes it difficult to establish genuine trust.
