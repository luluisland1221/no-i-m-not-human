Character Overview

The Suit Guy presents as a corporate professional, impeccably dressed and business-savvy. His perfect appearance and knowledge of business practices may represent either a legitimate businessman or a Visitor using corporate familiarity as a sophisticated disguise.

First Impression

Appears as a well-dressed business professional with perfect grooming

Key Behavior

Speaks in corporate jargon and business terminology

Professional Demeanor

Maintains perfect composure and business etiquette

Background Story

The Corporate Executive

The Suit Guy claims to be a corporate executive with connections to major business operations in the city. He speaks of board meetings, business acquisitions, and corporate restructuring as if they were everyday matters. His knowledge of business practices and corporate culture seems extensive and authentic.

The Business Network

Beneath the corporate facade lies the question of authenticity. He may be part of a business network that serves as a perfect cover for Visitor activities - corporations with legitimate fronts that can move freely, gather intelligence, and establish connections without raising suspicion.

The Perfect Mimic

The Suit Guy's understanding of business might be too perfect, too textbook. Visitors studying human commerce might have mastered the language and practices of business without truly understanding the underlying human elements - the passion, the risk, the personal investment that drives real entrepreneurs.
