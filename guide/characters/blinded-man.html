Character Overview

The Blinded Man is a character who presents himself with visual impairments and heightened awareness. His lack of sight gives him a distinctive perspective on the world, and his cautious nature makes him seem more vulnerable than other characters.

Visual Impairment

Claims to be blind or visually impaired, affecting navigation and perception

Background Story

Visual Impairment Background

The Blinded Man appears to have genuine visual impairments that significantly impact his daily life. His lack of sight suggests he may have experienced trauma, injury, or a progressive condition that led to blindness.

Purpose for Visiting

His reasons for seeking entry may relate to needing safe navigation assistance, escaping dangerous environments he cannot perceive, or seeking shelter where his visual impairment won't make him vulnerable.

Behavioral Adaptations

He maintains heightened sensory awareness and caution that compensates for his lack of sight, but this dependency on others might be masking his true abilities or hiding ulterior motives behind apparent vulnerability.
