Character Overview

The Reporter is a media professional who arrives seeking information about the strange supernatural events occurring in the building. This character represents the game's theme of outside attention and investigation into the protagonist's experiences.

Professional Appearance

Presents themselves as a legitimate journalist or reporter

Information Seeking

Shows strong interest in the supernatural events and visitor phenomena

Investigative Nature

May ask probing questions and seek detailed information

Background Story

Media Investigation

The Reporter may be investigating rumors of strange occurrences, missing persons, or supernatural activity in the area. Their presence suggests that the protagonist's situation has attracted outside attention.

Professional Motives

As a media professional, the Reporter may seek exclusive stories, evidence of supernatural events, or information about the various visitors that the protagonist encounters. Their true intentions may vary between genuine journalism and more sensational motives.
