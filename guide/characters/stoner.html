Character Overview

The Stoner appears as a laid-back, relaxed individual who seems to be in an altered state of consciousness. His behavior might be attributed to substance use, but it could also be something far more alien and terrifying.

Altered Perception

Seems to perceive reality differently than others

Laid-back Demeanor

Extremely relaxed and unconcerned about danger

Unusual Insights

May offer strange but oddly accurate observations

Background Story

The Counterculture Figure

The Stoner presents himself as someone who embraces alternative lifestyles and altered states of consciousness. He speaks of seeing things differently and understanding truths that others miss in their "normal" state.

Altered States or Alien Mind?

His altered perception could be the result of substance use, or it might be that his mind works on a completely different level. What appears to be drug-induced behavior might actually be the normal state of an entity that thinks in ways humans cannot comprehend.

The Perfect Disguise

In a world where paranoia is justified, the Stoner's relaxed demeanor and altered state serve as the perfect cover. If he slips up or reveals something inhuman, it can be dismissed as just another drug-induced hallucination.
