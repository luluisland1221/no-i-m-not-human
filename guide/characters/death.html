Character Overview

Death is the personification of mortality itself, appearing as a visitor to collect souls or maintain the natural order. This cosmic entity represents the ultimate supernatural encounter, testing players' understanding of reality itself.

First Impression

Appears as a traditional or unexpected interpretation of Death

Key Behavior

Speaks of mortality, fate, and the natural order of life and death

Cosmic Significance

Represents the fundamental force of mortality in the universe

Background Story

The Eternal Collector

Death has existed since the beginning of mortality, serving as the cosmic entity responsible for collecting souls and maintaining the balance between life and death. This entity appears when necessary to fulfill its ancient duties.

Purpose in the Game

Death's appearance suggests that the visitor situation has become significant enough to warrant cosmic attention. Whether it has come to collect specific souls, restore balance, or observe the situation depends on the context of the encounter.
