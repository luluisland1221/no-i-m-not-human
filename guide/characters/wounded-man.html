Character Overview

The Wounded Man appears with visible injuries that require immediate medical attention. His condition ranges from moderate to severe, suggesting he was involved in some traumatic incident. He may attempt to bribe or manipulate his way inside to seek shelter or medical help.

Background Story

Traumatic Incident

The Wounded Man appears to have survived a violent encounter or accident, with injuries suggesting he was attacked or caught in some disaster. His wounds may be from non-human sources, raising questions about what actually happened to him.

Desperate Escape

He claims to be fleeing from something or someone, using his injuries as justification for seeking immediate shelter. The desperation in his behavior suggests genuine fear or calculated manipulation.

Medical Crisis

His injuries may be more than they appear - potentially infected, supernatural in nature, or deliberately self-inflicted to gain sympathy and access. The need for medical help may be genuine or a pretext for gaining entry.
