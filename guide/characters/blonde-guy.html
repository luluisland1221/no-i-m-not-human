Character Overview

The Blonde Guy is a male visitor distinguished by his blonde hair. He represents one of the more ordinary-looking visitors that players might encounter, making identification more challenging due to his conventional appearance.

Background Story

Appearance at the Door

The Blonde Guy arrives with various reasons for seeking entry, ranging from everyday requests to more suspicious motives. His ordinary appearance makes his story more believable.

Like many visitors, the Blonde Guy's true nature is randomly determined. He may be genuinely human, a visitor in disguise, or something in between.
