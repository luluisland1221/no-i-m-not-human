Character Overview

The Fetus is a mysterious entity that appears in unborn form, representing themes of rebirth, potential, and supernatural transformation. This character challenges players' understanding of life and what constitutes a "visitor" in the game's universe.

First Impression

Appears as an elderly figure but represents something unborn and mysterious

Key Behavior

Speaks of rebirth, potential, and the cycle of life and death

Supernatural Nature

Represents the game's exploration of unconventional visitor forms

Background Story

Unborn Mystery

The Fetus appears in a form that suggests it represents something yet to be born or reborn. Its true nature may be connected to the game's deeper themes of transformation and the blurring lines between human and non-human existence.

Purpose for Seeking Entry

This entity may seek shelter to complete a transformation process, to be reborn into a new form, or to escape some cosmic force that threatens its unborn state. The reasons for its appearance are as mysterious as its nature.
