Character Overview

The FEMA Agent arrives as a figure of authority during the crisis, presenting official credentials and claiming to be following federal emergency management protocols. His presence immediately raises questions about government involvement and the true nature of the situation unfolding in the neighborhood.

First Impression

Appears professional, authoritative, and knowledgeable about emergency procedures

Key Behavior

Follows strict protocols and references official procedures frequently

Story Evolution

His official narrative may contradict other accounts of government actions

Background Story

The Official Assignment

He claims to be part of a federal response team deployed to manage the escalating crisis in the area. According to his story, he's following established emergency management protocols and conducting house-to-house assessments to ensure citizen safety and compliance with quarantine measures.

The Government Response

The FEMA Agent speaks of coordinated efforts between various agencies to contain what the government officially classifies as a public health emergency. He references evacuation orders, quarantine zones, and the importance of following official guidance for everyone's safety.

The Protocol Break

He explains that his presence at your door is due to either being separated from his team or following procedures that require him to seek temporary shelter while continuing his duties. His story involves checkpoints, supply distribution, and maintaining order in increasingly chaotic circumstances.
