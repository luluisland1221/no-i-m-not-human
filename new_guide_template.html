🔍 Daily Detection Methods

Each day brings new methods to identify Visitors. The news reveals new identification traits that you must use to distinguish between humans and impostors.

Day 1

🦷 Flawless White Teeth

Visitors have perfect, unnaturally white teeth. Check their smile carefully.

Detection Method: Ask them to smile or laugh naturally.

Day 2

✋ Nails

Visitor nails appear different from human nails - they may be too perfect or have unusual colors.

Detection Method: Observe their hands during conversation.

Day 3

👁️ Blood-Red Eyes

Visitors reveal blood-red eyes when stressed or when asked specific questions.

Detection Method: Ask challenging questions or shine a light.

Day 4

🦗 Hairless Armpits

Visitors lack natural body hair in their armpits - a clear biological difference.

Detection Method: Observe when they raise their arms.

Day 5

📸 Aura Photography

Special photography reveals unusual aura patterns around Visitors.

Detection Method: Use any camera or photography opportunity.

Day 7

🐛 Bugs in Ears

Visitors have small insects or parasites in their ear canals.

Detection Method: Close examination of their ears.

Day 8

🩸 Bleeding Gums

Visitor gums bleed spontaneously or when eating certain foods.

Detection Method: Observe during meals or dental questions.

Day 9

🔴 Skin Irritation

Visitors develop unusual skin rashes or irritations on specific days.

Detection Method: Check exposed skin areas.

Day 10

🍄 Fungus Growth

Unusual fungal growth appears in Visitors' armpits.

Detection Method: Discreet examination of armpit area.

Day 11

👀 Rapidly Moving Pupils

Visitor pupils move at unusual speeds when asked certain questions.

Detection Method: Ask rapid questions and observe eye movement.

⚙️ Core Game Mechanics

🏠 The Core Rule: Don't Be Alone

The fundamental rule of survival: you must always have people with you. Living alone makes you vulnerable.

Strategy: Always maintain at least one other person in your location.

⚡ Energy Management

Energy Costs:

  • Checking people for Visitor traits: Costs energy
  • Talking to people: Free (no energy cost)
  • Energy bars replenish over time
Optimization: Use conversation first, then spend energy on targeted checks.

📺 News System

The television provides crucial information each day:

  • Reveals new Visitor detection methods
  • Provides story updates and warnings
  • Shows FEMA agent movements
Importance: Always watch the news - it's vital for survival.

🚔 FEMA Agent Patterns

Government agents arrive periodically to take people away:

  • Follows specific schedule patterns
  • Can be delayed or avoided with proper timing
  • Key to certain story branches
Strategy: Learn their patterns and time your activities accordingly.

⚡ Advanced Energy Management

💡 Energy Optimization Tips

🗣️ Talk First, Check Later

Use conversation to gather initial information before spending energy on detailed checks.

🎯 Target Your Checks

Focus energy on suspicious individuals rather than random checking.

⏰ Time Your Examinations

Check people when energy bars are full or after energy-replenishing items.

📊 Keep Track of Results

Maintain mental notes of who you've checked and their results.
