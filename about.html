Our Story

The Beginning

It all started when we discovered "No, I'm not a Human" during its early access phase. Immediately captivated by the game's unique art style and psychological horror elements, we spent countless hours exploring every aspect of the gameplay. But we quickly realized this wasn't just another horror game – it was something special, something that demanded careful attention to detail.

The Challenge

As we delved deeper into the game, we encountered the complex character identification system, the intricate achievement requirements, and the multiple branching endings. We found ourselves taking detailed notes, comparing experiences, and testing different strategies. Soon, our personal documentation grew into something substantial.

The Evolution

What began as shared notes between friends transformed into a systematic approach to understanding every game mechanic. We spent months analyzing character behaviors, mapping out decision trees, and documenting every possible outcome. Our collective knowledge base became so comprehensive that we decided to share it with the wider gaming community.

The Mission

Today, our guide website serves as the definitive resource for "No, I'm not a Human" players worldwide. We continue to update our content with each game patch, incorporate community feedback, and explore new strategies. Our commitment to accuracy and comprehensiveness remains unwavering.
