Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What's the best strategy for identifying Visitors?

A: Focus on behavioral patterns and physical anomalies. Visitors often have unusual movements, strange requests, or inconsistent stories. Pay attention to their eyes, skin texture, and reaction to basic questions.

Q: How do I unlock the "Perfect Survivor" achievement?

A: Complete all 12 nights without allowing any Visitors inside, deny all visitors including the Cashier Girl, and ensure all humans survive. This requires careful character identification throughout the entire game.

Q: Are there multiple ways to achieve the same ending?

A: Yes, most endings have multiple paths. The key factors are which characters you allow inside, who survives, and what choices you make during critical events. Check our complete ending guide for all variations.

Q: What happens if 2+ Visitors get inside?

A: This triggers the worst ending scenario where all humans are killed. The game becomes significantly harder as Visitors work together to deceive you. Always deny suspicious characters.

Q: Is the Cashier Girl really a Visitor?

A: For the best ending strategy, yes. Many players report that denying the Cashier Girl, even though she appears innocent, leads to better outcomes. However, this is debated in the community.

Q: How can I support this guide?

A: Share it with fellow players, contribute your discoveries, and help us keep the information accurate and up-to-date. Community involvement makes this resource better for everyone.
