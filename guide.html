Complete Story Walkthrough

Phase 1: Days 1-3 - Understanding the Threat

1

Initial Setup

Begin by securing your shelter. Board up windows, check emergency supplies, and familiarize yourself with the basic controls. The first visitors will be relatively easy to identify.

Tips: Save frequently, use minimal energy on first investigations
2

Learning Visitor Traits

FEMA broadcasts will begin teaching identification methods. Pay close attention to descriptions of abnormal teeth, bloodshot eyes, and unusual hair growth patterns.

Tips: Take notes on broadcast information, some clues are time-sensitive

Phase 2: Days 4-7 - Rising Stakes

3

Increased Visitor Activity

Visitors become more frequent and sophisticated. Some may exhibit only partial traits or attempt to conceal their features. Energy management becomes critical.

Tips: Prioritize high-energy investigations for suspicious visitors
4

Resource Scarcity

Supplies begin running low. You'll need to balance investigation energy with resource gathering and shelter maintenance.

Tips: Keep backup supplies, don't neglect shelter repairs

Phase 3: Days 8-15 - Survival Endgame

5

Breaking Point

Government services collapse. You're truly alone. Visitors become desperate and more aggressive. Major story decisions will determine your fate.

Tips: Make save files before major decisions, consider long-term consequences
6

Final Confrontation

The truth about visitors begins to emerge. Your choices throughout the game will determine which ending you receive and the fate of your character.

Tips: Review all collected evidence, trust your instincts

üß† Advanced Detection Techniques

Psychological Evaluation

Ask personal questions about their past, family, or emotional experiences. Visitors often struggle with complex emotional responses or detailed human memories.

Effectiveness: High

Sensory Testing

Test reactions to various stimuli: strong smells, loud noises, temperature changes. Visitors may exhibit abnormal sensory responses.

Effectiveness: Medium

Consistency Checking

Ask the same questions in different ways. Visitors often contradict themselves or forget their fabricated stories.

Effectiveness: Very High

Physical Response Testing

Observe reactions to physical proximity, personal space invasion, or sudden movements. Visitors may have unnatural reflexes or lack of personal boundaries.

Effectiveness: Medium

All Endings Guide

No, I'm not a Human features multiple branching endings based on your decisions throughout the game. Each ending requires specific choices and conditions to unlock.

üèÜ

Survivor Ending

Requirements:

  • Correctly identify 90%+ of visitors
  • Maintain shelter integrity above 75%
  • Save at least 10 human lives
  • Collect all key evidence items
  • Reach Day 15 with positive reputation

Strategy:

Focus on accurate identification, be conservative with energy, and help genuine humans whenever possible. Keep detailed notes on visitor patterns.

üî¨

Discovery Ending

Requirements:

  • Investigate every visitor thoroughly
  • Find all hidden clues and documents
  • Decipher the visitor's true origin
  • Complete all research objectives
  • Confront the source of the invasion

Strategy:

Prioritize investigation over survival. Use energy on deep examinations, search every location, and piece together the complete story.

ü§ù

Community Ending

Requirements:

  • Help at least 15 different survivors
  • Build trust with repeat visitors
  • Establish communication networks
  • Share resources generously
  • Maintain positive relationships

Strategy:

Be compassionate and trusting. Help everyone you can, even if it means taking risks. Build a network of allies and share resources.

üíÄ

Tragic Ending

Requirements:

  • Make 5+ major identification errors
  • Allow critical shelter damage
  • Lose key allies through poor decisions
  • Fail to uncover essential information
  • Reach Day 15 with low survival rate

Strategy:

This ending occurs naturally through poor play. To avoid it, save frequently and learn from mistakes.

üé≠

Hidden Truth Ending

Requirements:

  • Complete specific dialogue choices with key NPCs
  • Find all hidden audio logs
  • Solve the radio frequency puzzle
  • Access the government bunker
  • Uncover the conspiracy

Strategy:

Pay attention to subtle clues, explore every dialogue option, and don't trust official broadcasts completely.

üåü

Sacrifice Ending

Requirements:

  • Save others at personal expense
  • Make selfless choices consistently
  • Sacrifice resources for the greater good
  • Protect vulnerable survivors
  • Face the final choice with compassion

Strategy:

Always put others before yourself. Make difficult choices that benefit the community even if they harm you personally.

General Tips for All Endings

Multiple Save Files: Keep different save files to explore various ending paths
Dialogue Matters: Your conversation choices affect relationships and story outcomes
Timing is Key: Some endings require specific actions on certain days
Reputation System: Your actions affect how others perceive and interact with you
Exploration Rewards: Thoroughly exploring each area reveals hidden endings
