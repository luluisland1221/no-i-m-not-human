📖 Prologue: The Beginning

The story begins as you find yourself alone in a world where "Visitors" - imposters who look human but aren't - roam among us. Your survival depends on identifying who is human and who is a Visitor.

I greeted him and asked how his wife and daughter were doing. He walked into my kitchen as if this was the most normal thing in the world. Then I checked the window - there was nothing outside, only an eerie silence.

The other doors were all locked, so I walked to my bedroom. I clicked on the bed and lay down, letting this day come to an end. As I gradually fell asleep, the news began to play, informing me of the current world situation.

When I woke up, I noticed two energy bars glowing in the corner of the screen. I got up and checked the radio on the table.

I carefully turned the tuning knob until the static signals disappeared, then listened to the emergency broadcast. After the broadcast ended, I went to the kitchen.

📝 Key Information Learned:

  • Core Game Mechanic: Always let others know you are not alone
  • Energy System: Checking people for Visitor traits consumes energy
  • Social Interaction: Talking with visitors doesn't consume energy
  • Survival Strategy: Check everyone for Visitor signs while maintaining energy

🌙 Night 1: First Encounters

Night falls again as I approach the door, ready to face the first real Visitors.

Neighbor's Daughter - This time it was the neighbor's daughter who appeared. She looked terrified. I asked her father's name, how she arrived here safely, and promised to help her find her father before wishing her good luck.

Tall Stranger - After she left, I checked the door again and found a tall stranger waiting.

I asked if he was a "Visitor," why he was expelled from the previous shelter, and finally invited him in. He walked quietly into the house.

Confused Woman - Now a woman appeared outside the door, claiming that "Visitors" had driven her from her home.

I asked how she came here, her expectations for the future, and finally told her to leave. Her story was unsettling, so I didn't allow her entry.

🌙 Night 2: Expanding Detection Methods

The second night brings new visitors and the first identification methods.

Delivery Person - The first knock of the night came from a delivery person (due to a previous day's order).

Among Us Guy - When I opened the door again, I met a guy making Among Us references. He wanted to party, but I refused.

Gravedigger - Next appeared a gravedigger holding a FEMA notice, also denied entry.

Shorts Hoodie Guy - Finally appeared a man wearing shorts and a hat. After conversation, I allowed him entry.

No more visitors that night. After checking the window, I went to bed. The night news provided the first clue for identifying Visitors: perfect white teeth.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Perfect White Teeth

Visitors have flawless, unnaturally perfect teeth. Check for this distinctive trait when examining visitors.

🌙 Night 3: Advanced Detection

With new detection methods revealed, face more sophisticated Visitors.

Pale Stranger - The pale stranger finally appeared. Facing the choice, I told them I was not living alone.

Hunter - Next appeared a rugged man in flannel shirt, claiming to hunt Visitors. I sent him away.

Bald Fortune Teller - Finally appeared a bald fortune teller who didn't want to enter but mentioned a hint about cats.

When I woke up the next morning, I found my energy bar had increased to 3 bars and received the third identification clue: blood-red eyes.

I checked the tall stranger's eyes in the hallway and the sunglasses man's eyes in the office.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Blood-Red Eyes

Visitors have distinctive blood-red eyes. Use this as a reliable identification method.

🌙 Night 4: FEMA Intervention

The government begins taking action. Navigate official intervention while maintaining survival strategy.

FEMA Agent - The first visitor was a FEMA agent. I asked about their protective equipment and action plan. Eventually they took the Medium Lady away.

Taxi Driver - The next visitor was a taxi driver. His vehicle was seized by FEMA and he needed a place to hide, but his history of attacking FEMA personnel was concerning, so I refused him entry.

Neighbor's Daughter - Then the neighbor's daughter appeared again. Her father was killed by a Visitor. Out of respect for him (the first person who cared about me), I allowed her entry.

Woman with Cat - The next visitor was a woman with a cat. Remembering the bald fortune teller's hint, I hesitated about allowing the cat in.

Her speech was confused, possibly XD or a Visitor. When I asked for proof of humanity, her response was even more suspicious. I refused entry and she left the cat under the house.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Hairless Armpits

Visitors have hairless armpits - a distinctive physical trait to identify them.

🌙 Night 5: Aura Photography

The fifth night introduces new identification methods and more challenging visitor decisions.

FEMA Agent - The first visitor was still a FEMA agent (new person who didn't know they had taken away a visitor before). This time they took the neighbor's daughter and left a FEMA notice.

Pale Stranger - The pale stranger appeared again. I told them I had done my best and was not living alone.

Scantily Clad Woman - The next woman was acting suspiciously. Despite her promises of favors, I refused her entry.

Mother and Son - A father who lost four children needed shelter. I allowed them entry.

After checking the window, I went to bed. The news announced a new identification method: aura photography.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Aura Photography

Special camera equipment can reveal Visitor auras that are invisible to the naked eye.

🌙 Night 6: Quiet Night

A relatively peaceful night with minimal visitor activity.

Only a delivery person visited that night. After checking the window, I found FEMA clearing escapees. I went to bed and waited for dawn.

The next morning I checked the TV and radio. The TV report added cigarettes to the delivery function menu.

I spoke with the guests in the house and took aura photos of the mother and son (results showed normal).

I checked their hands and eyes to confirm they were safe, then left. I drank coffee to gain +1 energy bonus for the next few days.

I called the grocery delivery phone to order 2 cans of energy drinks, ending the day.

🌙 Night 7: Insects and Disease

New identification methods emerge as the Visitor threat evolves.

Lawyer - The first visitor wanted to pay rent but was still refused.

Researcher - The next visitor was a researcher who didn't trust FEMA. They provided information about Visitor head parasites and were refused.

Sick Firefighter - The next visitor was clearly ill. Because they were too obviously like a Visitor and seemed near death, I allowed them entry and received a FEMA notice.

No more visitors that night. After checking the window, I went to bed. The news provided a new identification feature: insects in ears.

The next morning I checked the news and radio. I spoke with the firefighter and took aura photos to verify human identity.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Insects in Ears

Visitors have insects living in their ear canals - a gruesome but reliable identification method.

🌙 Night 8: Team Members and Bleeding Gums

Strange visitors arrive as more identification methods are revealed.

FEMA came again and took away two guests.

Team Member - The next visitor was a creepy team member. After conversation, they asked to accept their brother. Because they were too weird, I agreed.

Visitor Hunter - A lone gunman who had attacked the FEMA center appeared, asking about community conditions before leaving.

Strange Eye Woman - The next woman warned that creatures were feeding on burned corpses and demanded to stay away. I refused her energy drink bribe.

After checking the window and finding more FEMA activity, I went to bed. The news announced a new feature: bleeding gums.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Bleeding Gums

Visitors exhibit unexplained gum bleeding - another physical sign of their non-human nature.

🌙 Night 9: Skin Irritation

The threats become more sophisticated as new identification methods emerge.

The first visitor was a delivery person.

Pale Stranger - The pale stranger visited again. I still told them I was not living alone.

Confused Man - I tried to invite a man abandoned by his parents, but he was rejected because the tall stranger was in the hallway, so he was sent away.

FEMA Uniform Impersonator - Someone wearing FEMA uniforms stripped from corpses was refused.

Drunk Comedian - Although not funny, I allowed them entry to increase the number of people.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Skin Irritation

Visitors display abnormal skin reactions and irritations that distinguish them from humans.

🌙 Night 10: Armpit Fungus

FEMA activity increases as more disturbing Visitor traits are discovered.

FEMA Agent - FEMA agent appeared first, demanding to take two people (the comedian and the burn victim).

Team Leader - Appeared again, saying his followers were safe.

Visitor Hunter - Asked about neighborhood conditions and requested to see body parts. After showing eyes, they left.

After checking the window, I went to bed. The news mentioned a new feature: armpit fungus growth.

The next morning I checked TV and radio. Only the team member and tall man remained in the house. I checked the team member's armpits and found fungal growth.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Armpit Fungus

Visitors develop distinctive fungal growth in their armpit area - a clear sign of infection.

🌙 Night 11: Rapid Pupil Movement

As tensions escalate, the final identification methods are revealed.

Couple - A couple arrived that night but was refused.

Pale Stranger - Visited for the last time, asking if I had accepted any visitors. I chose "Have cleared all abnormal ones" and said I was not living alone.

Team Leader - Demanded to take his followers and provide death redemption. I refused.

Expelled Woman - Warned not to accept young men. I refused.

Angry Man - Condemned the world as sadists and cowards, tried to bribe with energy drinks but was refused.

🔬 New Identification Method:

Rapid Pupil Movement

Visitors exhibit unusual rapid eye pupil movement - a distinctive neurological sign.

🌙 Night 12: Government Changes

The political landscape shifts as FEMA consolidates power.

Visitor Hunter - Requested another check. After showing eyes, they left satisfied.

Teacher - The next visitor was refused at the door, demanding promises to care for abandoned children.

Headband Man - Claimed to hunt Visitors but was refused.

After checking the window, I went to bed. The news announced FEMA had become the temporary government. I noticed the energy bar had increased to 4 bars.

I spent the remaining energy checking myself in the bathroom for any Visitor traits.

  • Document Everything - Keep track of visitor behaviors
  • Strategic Referrals - Know when to deny entry
