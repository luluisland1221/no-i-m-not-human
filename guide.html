üéØ Core Rules

  • Never Solo Rule: Visitors never appear alone
  • Perfect White Teeth: Unnaturally perfect teeth indicate Visitor
  • Blood-Red Eyes: Red or glowing eyes are definitive Visitor signs
  • Hairless Armpits: Lack of body hair in armpit area
  • Nail Abnormalities: Unusual nail growth or color

‚ö° Energy Management

  • Save Items: Use save items before major decisions
  • Rest Periods: Balance checking with rest to maintain energy
