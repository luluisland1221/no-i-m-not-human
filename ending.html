Welcome to the most comprehensive guide for all endings in No, I'm not a Human! This paranoia-driven horror survival game features 10 unique endings that reveal different aspects of the apocalyptic story. üìã Endings Overview

No, I'm not a Human offers multiple endings based on your decisions throughout the 14-day survival period. The game's endings are influenced by:

  • Character Choices: Who you let into your house and how you interact with them
  • Visitor Identification: Your ability to correctly identify Visitors vs. Humans
  • Resource Management: How you use energy and items
  • Dialogue Options: Conversational choices that affect relationships
  • Timing: Specific actions must be performed on certain days

üéÆ Full Game Endings (Complete Walkthrough)

The complete version of No, I'm not a Human features 10 unique endings. Here's the complete guide to unlock each one:

Ending 1: The Child of Doom

üèÜ Achievement: Child of Doom

"A shot thundered through the milk of the fog, and above the crowd rose wings that reeked of ash."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Let the Seductive Woman enter your house
  2. Always choose the kindest, non-confrontational dialogue options with her
  3. On the 4th day after her arrival, she dies inevitably
  4. Interact with bedroom curtains and search the house to find the Fetus (spawns in 2 locations)
  5. Next night, observe the child drinking dog's milk
  6. Look through the peephole to trigger the ending
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Be extremely patient and nurturing with the Seductive Woman
  • Save frequently before key decisions
  • The Fetus can appear in different locations - search thoroughly

Ending 2: The End?

üèÜ Achievement: The End?

"The basement became a universe-windows bricked shut, voices merged with the ticking clock. The sun fades...but torment does not end so gently."

üìã Requirements:
  1. By Day 13, ensure only humans remain in the house
  2. Board up windows on Day 14
  3. Alternative: Being completely alone also triggers this ending
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Carefully identify and eliminate all Visitors before Day 13
  • Keep human guests safe and healthy
  • This ending represents the "good" survival scenario

Ending 3: No, I'm Not Alone

üèÜ Achievement: No, I'm Not Alone

"The Visitors were here all along-a warm breath in the dark, a cruel whisper at your back. You will never see the light again, should it ever return."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Keep at least one Visitor inside the house until Day 13-14
  2. Board up windows on Day 14
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Choose not to identify or eliminate a specific Visitor
  • Protect your chosen Visitor from other threats
  • This ending reveals the true nature of coexistence with Visitors

Ending 4: May Death Cleanse us of Our Sins!

üèÜ Achievement: Death Cult

"They prayed for the final silence. Perhaps their plea was finally heard- it's quiet out there."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Keep all Death Cult Peons alive in the house
  2. Don't kill them yourself - let Visitors kill them or let FEMA take them
  3. Wait for the Death Cult Leader's second visit
  4. Accept the offer to join the cult
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Protect the cult members at all costs
  • Be prepared to sacrifice other guests to save them
  • The cult represents a dark path to salvation

Ending 5: Welcome to FEMA

üèÜ Achievement: FEMA Agent

"They carried you away under sirens, leaving all your memories behind. If only you could return, just to take the poster with you."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Call FEMA every day once the phone number is available
  2. Watch all ads to get the FEMA phone number
  3. Always choose compliant options with the FEMA Agent
  4. Ask about packing and hazmat suit
  5. Give FEMA notices to confirmed visitors (Cashier Girl, Cat Lady)
  6. Don't question FEMA motives
  7. Accept the job offer when extended
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Be extremely cooperative with government authorities
  • Trust the system and follow instructions precisely
  • This represents the "establishment" ending

Ending 6: Yes, I'm Alone

üèÜ Achievement: Intruder

"The moment his shadow crossed the threshold, the house fell silent. Now all the remains of you are the whispers."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Tell the Intruder the protagonist is alone when he asks
  2. Alternative: Have no one in the house when he knocks
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • This ending can be achieved through isolation or deception
  • The Intruder represents external threats to survival
  • Consider the moral implications of your choice

Ending 7: Yes, I'm a Murderer

üèÜ Achievement: Murderer

"Everyone you met seemed an enemy. Fear blinded you, and others merely finished what you had already begun."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Kill a large number of humans with the gun
  2. Easiest method: Eliminate the group of cultists
  3. Egghead Guy appears to announce fate is inevitable
  4. Ending triggers after his visit
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • This represents the "paranoia gone wrong" path
  • Be prepared to face the consequences of your actions
  • The cult members are the easiest targets for this ending

Ending 8: Shroom or Doom

üèÜ Achievement: Mushroom

"Mushrooms rose from the soil, building foreign cities anew. You hurried, but the doors to their world were already closed."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Let Cat Lady in on the 4th Night to unlock the Basement
  2. Mushroom Man visits and gives Book of Smiles page
  3. Pick the basement mushroom, place it in fridge, eat it
  4. Next day, eat the apple that appears in the Hall (next to Toto toy)
  5. Third day, click the clock above the bed to set time to midnight
  6. Mushroom Man returns and invites you to Mushroom Land
  7. Accept the invitation
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • This is the most complex and surreal ending
  • Follow the sequence precisely - timing is crucial
  • Represents a psychedelic escape from reality

Ending 9: Embrace The Inevitable

üèÜ Achievement: Underground

"One heavy step across the line, and everything you feared loses its weight. Time has vanished, leaving only a coldness that feels like comfort."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Complete all of Egghead Guy's quests
  2. Let Cat Lady and her cat in
  3. Egghead appears on Night 5 - don't test anyone for 2 days (Nights 6-7)
  4. Dig in the basement 3 times (can use energy items)
  5. Find the tunnel, ignore Miner's warning about the hole
  6. Jump in the hole after Egghead's third visit
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • Trust the Egghead Guy and follow his guidance
  • This ending represents acceptance of the unknown
  • Have energy items ready for basement digging

Ending 10: Wrath of the Vigilante

üèÜ Achievement: Vigilante

"One trial ended, only for another to begin. Now, in the eyes of the crowd, you are the one to be feared."

üìã Requirements:
  1. Fail Vigilante's test on his second visit (Night 10 or 12)
  2. Choose wrong body part to show (teeth, hands, eyes, armpits, ears) or wrong auracamera photo
  3. Vigilante points gun, triggering ending
üí° Strategy Tips:
  • This ending requires deliberate failure of the Vigilante's test
  • Represents being judged by society's standards
  • Save before the Vigilante's second visit to explore different outcomes
