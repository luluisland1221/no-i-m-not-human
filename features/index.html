Visitor Identification System

Master the art of detecting Visitors disguised as humans through sophisticated physical trait analysis and behavioral observation mechanics.

Key Identification Clues:

  • Abnormal Teeth: Visitors often have perfectly white, flawless teeth that look unnatural
  • Bloodshot Eyes: Red streaks and unusual eye patterns reveal their true nature
  • Hair Patterns: Missing underarm hair or abnormal growth patterns
  • Behavioral Analysis: Subtle differences in speech patterns and reactions

The Challenge:

No single clue is definitive - some Visitors appear completely normal while some humans exhibit suspicious traits. Your judgment calls determine who lives and who dies.

Energy Management System

Every action in the game consumes precious daily energy points, forcing you to make strategic decisions about resource allocation.

Energy-Consuming Actions:

  • Appearance Checks: Close examination of physical features costs energy
  • Dialogue Investigations: Questioning visitors about their background
  • Background Research: Checking identification and documentation
  • Weapon Readiness: Preparing your shotgun for immediate threats

Strategic Depth:

Balance your investigation needs with survival requirements. Run out of energy and you'll be vulnerable when real threats arrive.

Shelter Management

Maintain your apocalyptic shelter as a safe haven while managing resources and providing for guests during the world-ending crisis.

Shelter Mechanics:

  • Resource Management: Food, water, and medical supplies
  • Guest Accommodations: Managing shelter space and visitor comfort
  • Security Systems: Reinforcing doors and windows against threats
  • Utility Maintenance: Keeping electricity and water running

Atmospheric Detail:

Watch through your windows as the apocalypse unfolds - spreading wildfires, military defeats, and societal collapse create an increasingly desperate world.

Multiple Game Endings

Experience branching storylines with randomized elements that create unique challenges and outcomes in each playthrough.

Ending Factors:

  • Shelter Judgments: Your decisions about who to admit or reject
  • Survival Rate: How many genuine humans you've saved
  • Morality Choices: The ethical implications of your decisions
  • Resource Management: How well you've maintained your shelter

Replayability:

Randomized visitor appearances, varying clue reliability, and multiple story paths ensure each playthrough offers new challenges and discoveries.
